What is Face Recognition?

Face recognition is a biometric technology that identifies or verifies a person based on a digital image or a video frame of their face. It's a sophisticated process that involves detecting a face in an image, analyzing its unique features, and comparing those features to a database of known faces.

Our app utilizes advanced algorithms to provide accurate and fast face analysis, offering insights and capabilities for various applications.
